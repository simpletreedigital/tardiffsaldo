Our Story

Built on the Central Coast, for the Central Coast

Tardiff & Saldo Law Offices was founded by Tyler Saldo and Dustin Tardiff, two attorneys who grew up on the Central Coast, went to law school, and came home to build a practice that reflects what their community actually needs.

Most law firms are either litigators or transactional attorneys. We are both. The contracts our business clients sign are drafted by attorneys who have argued contract breaches in court. The estate plans we create are structured by attorneys who have contested poorly drafted trusts in probate proceedings. That cross-pollination of experience makes every document we produce more durable.

The legal entity is Appellate Advocacy Group, which reflects another dimension of the firm's origin. Appellate work has always been part of the practice. The name Tardiff and Saldo is how the community knows us, and it is what we stand behind.
