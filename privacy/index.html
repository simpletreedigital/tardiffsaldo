This Privacy Policy describes how Tardiff and Saldo Law Offices ("we," "us," or "the Firm") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when you visit tardiffsaldo.com or contact us through our website, forms, phone, or text message.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you submit a contact form, request a consultation, or communicate with us, we may collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • A brief description of your legal matter or inquiry
  • Your SMS consent status if you opt in to text message communications

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we and our service providers may automatically collect:

  • Browser type, device type, operating system, and IP address
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referral source
  • Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager data used to understand website performance
  • Call tracking data when you call a tracked phone number displayed on the site

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies from Google (Analytics, Tag Manager, Ads) and GoHighLevel (CRM and chat widget). You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not function properly as a result.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule consultations
  • Send appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-up communications
  • Send non-marketing text messages if you have provided SMS consent (see Section 4)
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors use it
  • Comply with legal and professional obligations
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

3. Attorney-Client Privilege and Confidentiality

Important: Submitting a contact form or inquiry does not create an attorney-client relationship. Confidential information should not be shared until an attorney-client relationship has been formally established. If you have already retained the Firm, communications through our secure client channels are protected by attorney-client privilege.

4. SMS Text Message Communications

If you check the SMS consent box on our contact forms, you are consenting to receive non-marketing text messages from Tardiff and Saldo Law Offices about appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-up communications related to your inquiry.

  • Message frequency: Message frequency may vary based on your inquiry and appointment schedule.
  • Message and data rates: Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
  • To get help: Text HELP to any message you receive from us.
  • To opt out: Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from text messages. You will receive one confirmation message and no further texts.
  • Age requirement: You must be 18 years of age or older to consent to receive text messages.
  • No marketing texts: We do not send promotional or marketing text messages. Messages are limited to appointment and case-related communications.

Your consent to receive text messages is not a condition of retaining our services. Opting out of texts will not affect your ability to work with our Firm.

Our SMS communications are provided through GoHighLevel (Lead Connector). Your mobile number will be shared with GoHighLevel solely for the purpose of delivering messages you have requested. GoHighLevel's privacy policy is available at gohighlevel.com/privacy-policy.

5. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: GoHighLevel (CRM and SMS), Google (Analytics, Ads, Tag Manager), and Cloudflare (website hosting). These providers process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of firm assets, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • With your consent: For any other purpose with your explicit consent.

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and inquiry records for a period consistent with California Rules of Professional Conduct and applicable statutes of limitation. SMS consent records are retained for a minimum of five years. You may request deletion of your information by contacting us at hello@tardiffsaldo.com, subject to our legal and professional obligations to retain certain records.

7. California Privacy Rights

California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information (subject to exceptions)
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@tardiffsaldo.com or (888) 461-2215. We will respond within 45 days.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your personal information. Our website is served over HTTPS. Contact form submissions are transmitted via encrypted connections to our CRM provider. No method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party websites including government agencies, courts, and legal resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at hello@tardiffsaldo.com.

11. Changes to This Policy
