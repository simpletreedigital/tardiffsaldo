Do Paso Robles residents need a living trust?+
In most cases, yes. Any California resident who owns real estate or has assets over $184,500 in their own name should seriously consider a living trust to avoid probate. California probate can take over a year and costs a statutory percentage of the gross estate value. A properly funded living trust avoids all of that and keeps your affairs private.
