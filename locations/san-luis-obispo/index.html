Do San Luis Obispo residents really need a living trust?+
In most cases, yes. California probate can take over a year and cost thousands of dollars in court fees based on your estate's gross value, not what it's worth after debts. For homeowners in SLO, where property values are significant, a living trust that avoids probate typically pays for itself many times over. It also keeps your estate private and lets assets transfer directly to your family without court involvement. We say "most everyone needs a trust" because we've seen the alternative too many times in court.
