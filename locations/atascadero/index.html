Do Atascadero property owners need a living trust?+
In most cases, yes. Any California resident who owns real estate should seriously consider a living trust to avoid probate. California probate applies to estates above $184,500 in individual-name assets, and the statutory fees are calculated on gross asset value, not equity. A $900,000 home with a $400,000 mortgage is still a $900,000 probate asset. Given current Atascadero and North Central SLO County property values, most homeowners are well above that threshold. We offer flat-fee trust packages and free consultations.
